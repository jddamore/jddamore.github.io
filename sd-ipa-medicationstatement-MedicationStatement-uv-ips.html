      descriptionMinimum expectations for a MedicationStatement resource when accessed via a International Patient Access API. The MedicationStatement resource represents all medications a patient is taking, be it those ordered by a clinician or reported by another organization, the patient or a caregiver.This profile represents the constraints applied to the MedicationStatement resource by the International Patient Summary (IPS) FHIR Implementation Guide, based on FHIR R4. A record of a medication statement is represented in the patient summary as an instance of a MedicationStatement resource constrained by this profile.
